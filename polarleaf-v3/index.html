Stand with a family

Behind every badge is a family who needs us now

The Polar Leaf was founded in 2017 by PBGen. Regino S. Catiis (Ret.) — a police officer and leukemia survivor. Today we are the first foundation in the Philippines built specifically for PNP and AFP personnel and their dependents facing blood cancers.

We walk with families through real help: medicine, transfusions, chemotherapy support, counseling, and a community that never lets a family face the journey alone.
