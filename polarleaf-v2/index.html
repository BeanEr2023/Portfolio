Programs of care, from aid to counseling

What your support makes possible

01 Financial assistance

Help with medical expenses, treatment, and benefit claims for officers, soldiers, and dependents battling blood cancers.

02 Awareness & education

Spreading knowledge of leukemia and blood disorders — symptoms, early detection, and accessible treatment across the PNP and AFP.

03 Medical & palliative support

Voluntary medical services and palliative-care guidance for patients and families through every stage of the journey.

04 Spiritual care & counseling

Counseling and spiritual management for patients and loved ones — Deus Spes Nostra, God is our hope.

05 Linkage & registry

Connecting with cancer foundations here and abroad to share donors, registries, and best practices in treatment.
