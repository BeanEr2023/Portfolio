Who We Are

A foundation born from service and survival

"A life lived for others is a life worth living."

The Polar Leaf was founded in 2017 by PBGen. Regino S. Catiis (Ret.) — a career officer of the Philippine National Police and a survivor of Acute Myeloid Leukemia. During his own battle and bone-marrow transplant, he saw how leukemia and blood disorders quietly devastate fellow officers, soldiers, and their families.

It is the first health foundation in the Philippines created specifically for PNP and AFP personnel and their dependents facing blood cancers — offering awareness, education, financial aid, and voluntary medical and spiritual support throughout their journey.

What We Do

How we help the fight

🩸

Financial Assistance

Aid for medical expenses, treatment, and benefit claims for PNP & AFP personnel and dependents battling blood cancers.

📣

Awareness & Education

Spreading knowledge on leukemia and blood disorders — symptoms, early detection, and accessible treatment within the PNP and AFP.

🩺

Medical & Palliative Support

Voluntary medical services and palliative-care guidance for victims and their families throughout their journey.

🙏

Spiritual Care & Counseling

Spiritual management and counseling for patients and loved ones — Deus Spes Nostra, God is our hope.

🤝

Linkage & Registry

Connecting with cancer foundations here and abroad to share donors, registries, and best practices in treatment.

💛

Fund-Raising

Solicitations and events that sustain the foundation and directly support victims' medical needs.
